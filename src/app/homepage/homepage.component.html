ABOUT US

From the Streets to a Safe Haven

Jamghat was born out of a theatre workshop with street children in 2003. What started as a play became a movement to provide a permanent home and identity to children who had nowhere to go.

We believe that every child deserves a childhood free from fear, hunger, and exploitation. Our holistic approach focuses not just on shelter, but on emotional healing, education, and skill development.
