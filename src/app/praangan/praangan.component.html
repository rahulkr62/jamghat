Praangan – Community Outreach

Engaging children and families in street and slum communities

Praangan is Jamghat's outreach programme engaging children and families in their street and slum communities. Conducted six days a week, it offers remedial classes, theatre, visual arts, counselling, and regular parent interactions.

location_on Programme Locations

Currently, the Praangan model is being run in five locations in Delhi: Shastri Park, Mithaipul, Takiya Kale Khan, Kalyanpuri, and Aya Nagar.

groups Daily Reach & Impact

Approximately 300 children (aged 6–18 years) participate in activities across these centres every day, making it one of Jamghat’s most active and impactful interventions.
